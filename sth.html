The Spiral

If you’re a lover of the feeling of invisibility than you’d definitely enjoy this hidden monument. No maps will show you how to get there, only these instructions: When you’re leaving the dormitory, don’t take the bus, go the other way and find the wooden paths in the forest. They will lead you to the playground. Take a turn right and you’re on the right way.
